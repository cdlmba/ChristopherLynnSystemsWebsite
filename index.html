Financial Governance

Institutional Resilience & Finance

A volunteer-led association faced "institutional fragility" due to fragmented, opaque financial processes. We shifted the focus from bookkeeping to governance design, codifying end-to-end workflows and clarifying decision logic.

The Result

Accelerated governance discussions through clear visibility and reduced onboarding friction for future leadership.
